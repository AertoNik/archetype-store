Common Inquiries

Do I need any coding or advanced Google Sheets skills?

Absolutely not. Every ARCHETYPE framework is a "plug-and-play" system. All complex Apps Script automations are pre-configured. You will receive a simple deployment guide to initialize your environment in less than 2 minutes.

How is my personal data protected?

Total data sovereignty is our core principle. Unlike standard apps, your data never touches our servers. The entire engine lives and breathes within your personal Google Drive. You are the only person with access to your logs, stats, and files.

Is the system optimized for mobile use?

Yes. While the initial setup is best done on a desktop, all modules are fully compatible with the official Google Sheets app for iOS and Android. You can log your progress, check your stats, and manage operations from anywhere in the world.

Is this a one-time purchase or a subscription?

ARCHETYPE follows a "Legacy Ownership" model. There are no recurring monthly fees. A single purchase grants you a permanent license to the framework, including all minor stability patches for that version.

What happens if Google updates their platform?

Our engineering team monitors Google Workspace API changes. If a major platform update affects the core logic of our frameworks, we provide documented fixes or updated versions to ensure your system remains operational.

How do I receive the framework after the transaction?

The deployment is instant. Immediately after payment, you will be redirected to a secure access page with a unique link. This link will authorize the transfer of the system directly into your Google Drive environment.
