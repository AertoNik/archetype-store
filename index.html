Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a paid Google Drive subscription?

No. ARCHETYPE systems are designed to run flawlessly on any free Google account. No hidden fees or recurring subscriptions are required.

Is this a one-time payment or a subscription?

This is a strictly one-time purchase. Once you set up the framework, you own the asset permanently, including all future minor patches for your specific version.

Can I operate the system from my smartphone?

Yes. All our modules are fully integrated with the free Google Sheets application for iOS and Android, allowing you to log data and track your progress on the go.

How do I receive the product after payment?

Upon a successful transaction, you will instantly receive a secure link. Clicking it will automatically generate a private copy of the system directly into your personal Google Drive.
