1. Introduction and Data Sovereignty

At ARCHETYPE ("we", "our", or "us"), we prioritize your digital sovereignty. Our core products are frameworks built on Google Sheets and Google Apps Script. We do not have access to, nor do we track, store, or monitor the personal data you enter into your purchased frameworks. All your operational data resides exclusively within your personal Google Drive account.

2. Information We Collect

While we do not access your framework data, we do collect limited information necessary to process your purchase and provide support:

  • Contact Information: Your email address, used exclusively for delivering the product, sending critical system updates, and providing customer support.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by our third-party payment gateways (e.g., Stripe, Gumroad). We do not store or have access to your full credit card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your technical support requests.

Our frameworks require a Google Account to function. By using our products, you are also subject to Google's Privacy Policy regarding how they handle data stored in Google Drive. We do not share your email or purchase history with any third parties, except for the payment processors required to complete your transaction.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at support@archetype.com.
