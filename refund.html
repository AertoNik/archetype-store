Refund Policy

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 2026

1. Digital Product Nature

All products sold by ARCHETYPE are digital goods (Google Sheets templates, Apps Script code, and instructional documentation). Because digital goods are delivered instantly and can be copied to your personal drive, they cannot be "returned."

2. General Refund Rule

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales are final and non-refundable once the secure access link has been generated and the file has been copied to your Google Drive.

3. Exceptions

We stand by the quality of our engineering. Refunds or replacements may only be considered under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate Transaction: If you were accidentally charged twice for the same product, we will refund the duplicate charge.
  • Technical Failure: If the file is completely inaccessible or fundamentally broken immediately upon purchase, and our support team is unable to provide a working version or fix the issue within 72 hours of your report.

4. Before Requesting a Refund

We ask that you contact our support team if you encounter any difficulties deploying your framework. Most issues (such as Google Apps Script authorization errors) are easily resolved through our standard setup protocols.

5. Contacting Support

To report a duplicate charge or technical failure, please email support@archetype.com with your order number and a clear description of the issue.
